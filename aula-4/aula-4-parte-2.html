Gráficos

Na Figura 1, utilizamos os pacotes palmerpenguins e tidyverse (HORST; HILL; GORMAN, 2022; WICKHAM et al., 2019), e o software R (R CORE TEAM, 2024):

library(tidyverse)
library(palmerpenguins)

penguins |>
  ggplot() +
  aes(x = flipper_length_mm, y = body_mass_g) +
  geom_point(aes(color = species, shape = species)) +
  scale_color_manual(values = c("darkorange", "darkorchid", "cyan4")) +
  theme_minimal()
